Who We Are

  • Amanos was created from over 20 years of working with and designing concrete and limestone pieces. Our proprietary method of finishing and patina allows us to create custom pieces that in the past, could only be found, not made.

  • AMANOS specializes in creating unique limestone planters, troughs, and firepits that can't be found anywhere else. Our unique processes achieve an antique limestone appearance while maintaining quality craftsmanship that can be depended on.

  • Each piece is formed partly in a mold and partly by hand. Its a process we have developed to make sure we can keep the shape we want, but if needed help create some of the imperfections we love. This makes every piece unique. No two are alike. After curing we hand chisel the entire surface of each piece.

  • In whole, each piece is a 3 day process. There are 3 steps we go through, forming/molding, chiseling, and patina/sealing.

  • One of the main reasons that people choose AMANOS is because we have the ability to make custom shapes, sizes and finishes according to your needs.

  • Check out our contact page to get in touch with us to get a conversation going about your custom piece.
